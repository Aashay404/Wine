Vitality Jamun, Jamun Wine

Rich dark fruit. Bold, dry elegance.

Jamun was a part of childhood in this part of the country in a way that is hard to explain to someone who did not grow up here. The deeply dark stain on your fingers. The sharp, slightly sweet, slightly earthy taste. The feeling of having eaten something real. Vitality Jamun captures all of that and gives it a structure it never had in a basket. Bold and dry, with a mineral depth that surprises first time drinkers. It does not try to be elegant. It is what it is: grounded, proud, and genuinely unlike any wine you have had before.

Traditional Methodology

Crafting the Jamun Vintage

01. Harvest

Jamun is sourced directly from growers within the network of Sahyadri Farms, harvested at optimal ripeness to retain its natural colour, tartness, and depth of flavour.

02. Fermentation

The fruit is carefully crushed and fermented under controlled conditions to preserve its distinctive dark fruit character, soft tannins, and natural balance.

03. Maturation

The wine is rested in temperature-controlled cellars, allowing its texture to round out and the finish to become smooth, structured, and elegantly dry.
