Pomegranate Wine
Prep: 10 mins Easy

The Crimson Spiced Sangria

A rich, festive interpretation of the Spanish classic. The deep red-fruit notes of our Pomegranate Wine blend seamlessly with fresh autumn citrus and a warm touch of cinnamon spice.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bottle (750ml) Sam's Pomegranate Wine
  • 60ml Brandy or Dark Rum
  • 1 Fresh Orange (sliced into wheels)
  • 1 Red Apple (cored and chopped)
  • 2 Cinnamon sticks
  • 150ml Club soda (to top)

Instructions

  1. Place the sliced oranges, chopped apples, and cinnamon sticks at the bottom of a large glass pitcher.
  2. Pour in the brandy and Sam's Pomegranate Wine, then stir gently.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours (ideally overnight) to allow the fruit flavors to infuse.
  4. Before serving, stir in the chilled club soda. Serve in red wine glasses filled with ice.
Jamun Wine
Prep: 5 mins Easy

Jamun Twilight Cooler

A refreshing twilight cooler that features the complex tartness and deep purple hues of Jamun Wine. Spiced with a hint of black salt and balanced with lime and fresh mint.

Ingredients

  • 120ml Sam's Jamun Wine
  • 6-8 Fresh Mint leaves
  • 2 Lime wheels
  • 15ml Simple syrup (optional)
  • A pinch of Black Salt (Kala Namak)
  • Tonic water (to top)

Instructions

  1. In a tall highball glass, gently muddle the mint leaves, lime wheels, and simple syrup to release the oils.
  2. Fill the glass to the brim with crushed ice.
  3. Pour over Sam's Jamun Wine and sprinkle the pinch of black salt.
  4. Top with chilled tonic water, stir gently from the bottom, and garnish with an extra sprig of fresh mint.
Mahua Wine
Prep: 8 mins Medium

Mahua Honeyed Elixir

Celebrating the ancient spirit of Mahua blossoms. This luxurious drink infuses honey, fresh ginger, and lime with the honeyed warmth of Mahua Wine. Can be served cold or as a warm toddy.

Ingredients

  • 90ml Sam's Mahua Wine
  • 15ml Fresh Ginger juice
  • 15ml Honey syrup (1:1 honey and warm water)
  • 20ml Fresh Lemon juice
  • Club soda (cold option) OR 90ml hot water (warm option)
  • Lemon twist (garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a shaker filled with ice, combine Mahua Wine, fresh ginger juice, honey syrup, and lemon juice.
  2. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds until well chilled, then strain into a rocks glass over a large ice sphere.
  3. Top with a splash of club soda and garnish with a lemon twist.
  4. For Warm Serving: Stir all ingredients (except club soda) in a heatproof mug with 90ml of hot water. Garnish with a cinnamon stick or lemon wheel.
Strawberry Wine
Prep: 6 mins Easy

Strawberry Basil Breeze

A bright, aromatic mix that highlights the sweet, freshly-crushed berry character of Strawberry Wine, elevated with fresh green basil leaves and a sparkling finish.

Ingredients

  • 120ml Sam's Strawberry Wine
  • 3-4 Fresh Basil leaves
  • 15ml Fresh Lemon juice
  • 3 Sliced fresh Strawberries
  • Sparkling water or Prosecco to top

Instructions

  1. Place the fresh basil leaves and sliced strawberries at the bottom of a large wine glass.
  2. Gently press them with a spoon or muddler to release juice and aromatics.
  3. Fill the glass halfway with cubed ice, then pour in the fresh lemon juice and Sam's Strawberry Wine.
  4. Top with a splash of sparkling water or Prosecco for extra effervescence. Garnish with a whole strawberry on the rim.
