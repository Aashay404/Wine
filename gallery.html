Educational Excellence

We recently had the pleasure of hosting students from Garti College for an immersive educational tour. The visit aimed to provide students with a deep dive into the world of viticulture and modern winemaking processes.

During their exploration, students visited our Orchardss to understand soil health and grape varieties, followed by a detailed walk-through of our fermentation and aging cellar. The day concluded with a sensory appreciation session, where they learned to identify the unique notes that define our collection.

Nature's Basket Representation

SAMWINE was proudly represented at the Nature's Basket premier wine showcase. This event brought together the finest labels under one roof, and our collection stood out as a highlight for connoisseurs and casual tasters alike.

We showcased our major collections, including our signature Ruby Rich and Reserve range. Our sommeliers engaged with guests, sharing the stories of our heritage and the specific climate conditions that make our wines unique. It was a celebration of nature's finest fruits captured in a bottle.
