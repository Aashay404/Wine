Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell wine directly through the website?

Currently, SamWine focuses on showcasing wine collections, wine culture, and curated content. Direct purchasing options may be introduced in the future.

Is there a membership or wine club?

At this time, SamWine does not operate a paid membership program. Future curated wine experiences may be announced.

What is the legal age requirement?

You must be of legal drinking age in your country to access this website.

How can I contact SamWine?

You can reach us through our contact form or email us at info@samwine.com.

Do you share personal information?

No. We do not sell or trade personal data. Information is used strictly to improve user experience and communication.
