Ruby Rich Reserve, Pomegranate Wine

Ruby Depth, Naturally Refined

They go to Switzerland. They go to the UK. They meet food safety standards that most Indian produce never gets tested against. They are grown on certified farms, harvested at exactly the right time, and handled like something precious because they are. Each bottle reflects the orchard-first philosophy associated with growers connected to Sahyadri Farms, celebrating authenticity and craftsmanship in every pour. Ruby Rich Reserve is deep and layered in a way that grape wine sometimes takes decades to achieve. There is a natural tartness to pomegranate that gives the wine real structure. There is a richness that lingers. And underneath it all there is the clean, honest taste of fruit that was grown right and touched as little as possible.

The Alchemy

From Orchard to Glass

01.Precision Harvest

Select Bhagwa pomegranates are handpicked from carefully managed Nashik Orchards at peak maturity to ensure vibrant colour, concentrated flavour, and natural freshness.

02. Fermentation

The fruit is softly crushed and fermented in controlled small batches, preserving its bright aromatics, delicate tannin structure, and true varietal character.

03.Patient Maturation

Rested under monitored conditions, the wine slowly harmonises acidity and texture, resulting in a smooth palate and refined finish that defines Sam’s Wine craftsmanship.
