Three Generations

We grew fruit for the world for 25 years.
Then we decided to pour it into a glass for you.

In 1996, my father started Sam Agri with one straightforward goal: grow better fruit than anyone else in India, and get it to the world in perfect condition. Pomegranates going to Swiss supermarkets. Figs landing in London. Coconut reaching kitchens in the UAE. For 25 years, that was the work.But there was always this quiet thought sitting in the back of everything. The best fruit we grew never left the farm. It stayed right here in Nashik. And one day we stopped asking why we were not doing more with it.
