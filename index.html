Sam's Wine

Crafted with Time & Tradition

Est. 2022

We grew fruit for the world for 25 years.
Then we decided to pour it into a glass for you.

Back in 1996, Mr. G.V.K. Naidu and his friends started Sam Agri with a simple goal: to grow the best fruit in India and deliver it fresh to the world. For 25 years, we did exactly that—sending pomegranates to Swiss supermarkets and fresh coconuts to kitchens in the UAE. But we always had one quiet thought. The very best fruit we grew never actually left the farm—it stayed right here in Nashik. One day, we decided it was time to do something more with it.
