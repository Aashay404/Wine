Crimson Silk, Strawberry Wine

Luscious berry essence, smooth seductive finish.

Peak season strawberries. Limited batches. The kind of bright, joyful flavour where sweet meets tart and makes you want to share a bottle the moment you open it. Crimson Silk is the wine you bring to a birthday. The one you open on a random Tuesday because the weather is nice. The one guests reach for first and ask about second. Not every bottle needs to carry the weight of history. Sometimes it just needs to make the evening better. This one does that very well.

Artisan Craft

The Seduction of Strawberry Wine

01. Hand-Selection

Only the plumpest, heritage-grade strawberries are selected from our organic Nashik partners at the peak of ripeness.

02. Gentle Infusion

A slow maceration process ensures the delicate floral notes and bright red pigments are naturally preserved.

03. Refined Finishing

The vintage is clarified through traditional gravity-fed methods to maintain its signature silky texture and clarity.
